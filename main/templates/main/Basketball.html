  • What is Basketball?

    Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court, usually indoors. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal, an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket.

  • What equipment do you need for Basketball?

    Basketball is a simple game, it doesn't require any equipment other than a hoop, a ball and a flat court. Even two players can play and have fun with basketball.

  • What should you wear to play Basketball?

    You'll need warmup suits and jackets for pre-game, and comfortable basketball shirts, T-shirts and practice jerseys, and it's always a good idea to keep a basic fleece hoodie in your locker. Fortify yourself on the court with compression layers — like shorts and tanks or shooter sleeves and leg sleeves.
